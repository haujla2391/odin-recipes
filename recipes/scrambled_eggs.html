Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe is one that I utilize every morning. It is of scrambled eggs with some turkey and cheese. This recipe was inspired by my university, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, where they make all types of scrambled eggs every morning.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 3 slices turkey
  • 1 cup spinach

Steps

  1. Spray oil on heated pan
  2. Add all eggs to pan
  3. Add the slices of turkey in small bits
  4. Add spinach in bits
  5. Add cheese
  6. Use spatula to mix up the contents and cook until done
