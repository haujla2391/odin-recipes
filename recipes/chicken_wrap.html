Chicken Wrap

Description

This recipe is one that I utilize every evening. It is of a chicken wrap with tomatoes and onions. This recipe was inspired by a YouTuber named Noel Deyzel. He made a chicken wrap recipe that was high in protein and relatively simple for anyone to make, even myself.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken strips *can make them yourself detailed below

    • **Making your own chicken strips**

    1. Remove fat from chicken strips
    2. Wash under some water
    3. Season and mix in your choice of seasoning
    4. Add oil to pan
    5. Lay chicken on pan and cook until done

  • Half an onion
  • Half a tomato
  • Choice of sauce
  • Tortilla
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Chop up onion and tomato
  2. Add oil to pan
  3. Add chopped onion and tomato to pan
  4. Take out onions and tomato when cooked, add tortilla
  5. Put chicken, onion, tomato, and cheese into tortilla
  6. Add sauce and enjoy!
