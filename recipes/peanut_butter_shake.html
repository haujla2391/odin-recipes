Peanut Butter Shake

Description

This recipe is one that I utilize every afternoon. It is of a peanut butter shake with honey, milk and oats. This recipe was inspired by my local gym where they offer this shake as a post workout drink. I decided to try to replicate this shake at home to save some money and improve my skills in the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon greek yogurt
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Pour oats in the blender
  2. Add honey and peanut butter to blender
  3. Add the greek yogurt to the blender
  4. Pour in the milk
  5. Blend and enjoy!
